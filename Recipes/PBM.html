Paneer Butter Masala

Description

A rich and creamy North Indian dish made with soft paneer cubes simmered in a buttery tomato-based gravy. It's a crowd favorite, often enjoyed with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves)
  • Garam Masala
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Cream
  • Oil
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, and cook until oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add paneer cubes, salt, and garam masala. Mix well.
  6. Pour in some water and let it simmer until the gravy thickens.
  7. Add cream and kasuri methi. Mix and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.
