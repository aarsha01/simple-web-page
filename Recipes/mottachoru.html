Ingredients

  • Oil
  • salt
  • masala(Chilly,Coriander,,Jeera)
  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Chilly

Steps:

  1. Heat up the oil little bit
  2. Add chilly and Ginger-Garlic paste
  3. Add chopped onion and fry until it becomes light brown
  4. Add some masala and mix it well
  5. Add 2 eggs and mix until its cooked
  6. Add rice and mix it well and then add some salt for taste
